About Us

History

Here at Durham House of Pets (DHP), we are a company that strives to keep customers and pets happy and healthy through our streamlined systems of documenting and maintaining pet records. Founded in August of 2014, co-owners Ben and Jerry Smith, have taken great pride in keeping both the store and website up to date with the most current information, news, and products. They work diligently with their staff to ensure that the company runs smoothly on a day to say basis and is set up for long-term success.

Why Use Us?

Our employees strive to meet each and every customers needs to ensure satisfaction and ease of sale in the store. Here at Durham House of Pets we pride ourselves in keeping a clean and welcoming store that we’re sure you’ll enjoy. We are the leading pet store in the Durham/Seacoast area, so come in and let us show you what sets us apart from our competitors.
