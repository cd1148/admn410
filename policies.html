Policies

Financing

In terms of financing, we started the business by finding loans that would cover the cost of the start-up process. Those have since been paid off, and the business is running self sufficiently at this point. We sell the product in a first in last out procedure to ensure that perishables like the dog food get sold before they expire.

Delivery

We do not have an online store, however when products are not in stock and need to be customer ordered for a customer, we send them directly to the customers house using FedEx.

Return Policies

Returns can be made within 30 days with proof of purchase and original tags attached.
